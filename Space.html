Unity game

Arcade
This game about space war:your hero need kill to all enemies,for save the world.

Hero

Hero can fly in space and fire to enemies.He has shield and gun.

Bonus Info
Blaster This weapon inflicts small damage ,but if you have 4-5 guns... It shoots only forward in a straight line.
Laser It is weapon is better for kill enemies level 3-4, because that gun has high damage,but small radius for attack.
Shread If you need kill a lot of enemy ,you can use Shread .That gun inflicts small damage,but three shells that fly diagonally and straight.
Shield Shield save your life and help to you end the game.

Enemies

Enemies are space ships from Aliense army.They want to recreate this world with out people and animals.

Enemy Info Feature
Level 1 It is infantry,this stupid enemy ship fly only forward in a straight line. Has 10 points healts. None
Level 2 That ship can fly forward in a straight line and dodge then to the right then to the left .Also has 10 points healts. Migration plan
Level 3 Appears where you do not expect,has 10 points healts. It can fly from the right front, or the left
Level 4 It has a rather interesting shape of movement, somewhat reminiscent of a sine wave. Interesting shape of movement
Level 5 The most difficult enemy, consisting of four parts, each must be destroyed. In general he has 40 points healts. Consisting of four parts
